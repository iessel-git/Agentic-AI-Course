Module 6: Testing & Evaluation

Ensure your AI agents are reliable, ethical, and effective

Why Testing Matters

Agentic AI must be rigorously tested to ensure safety, accuracy, and alignment with human goals. Evaluation helps verify if agents behave as intended across different tasks and environments.

Evaluation Methods

  • Unit Testing: Verifies components in isolation.
  • Behavioral Testing: Observes real-world performance of the agent.
  • Simulation: Tests agent interactions in virtual environments.
  • Human-in-the-loop Testing: Incorporates feedback and control from human users.

Ethical Evaluation

Agents should be evaluated not only for technical success but also ethical impact. Consider fairness, transparency, and unintended consequences.
