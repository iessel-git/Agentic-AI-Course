Module 3: Agent Architecture

Explore how AI agents are structured and interact with the world

Common Architectures

  • Reactive agents: respond to environment with no internal state
  • Deliberative agents: maintain beliefs, goals, and plans
  • Hybrid agents: combine reactive and deliberative strategies

Perception, Memory, and Actuation

An agent typically includes components for sensing inputs, storing knowledge, making decisions, and acting. Understanding this flow is key to designing effective AI systems.
